Frameworks

This Consortium Project is divided into a number of different disciplines, referred to here as “Frameworks”. Although there is some agreement between individual Frameworks and the participating Institutions, this is rather an artificial correspondence. In reality, research staff may well work in several different Frameworks, collaborating with staff from other institutions at different times. For this reason, the description of the work is broken down into different Frameworks, but the staff I have listed under Research Staff, Project Partners and Canadian Partners are grouped by institution.
