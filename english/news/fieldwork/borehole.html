Borehole

We’ve now completed the drilling programme and have cores for 500 metres of rock from near Berwick upon Tweed. Sadly, we didn’t succeed in getting through the entire Ballagan Formation, which seems to be rather thicker than we’d calculated. Still, here’s a YouTube compilation put together by Tim Kearsey from BGS Edinburgh to give you a flavour of what they’ve been up to. There is no sound track to this video. Tim has also posted about the drilling programme and given lots more interesting information on the BGS GeoBlogy blog.
