Fieldwork

This entire project is based on some really interesting fossils which Tim Smithson and Stan Wood found in the Borders Region of Scotland, and many of the localities where these fossils came from are still yielding good material.  As you might expect, we keep going back for more!

In addition, we’ve started to hear about other sites in the UK which look promising, and which merit a visit to see whether they yield useful material, so as we do so, we’ll report what we find here.
